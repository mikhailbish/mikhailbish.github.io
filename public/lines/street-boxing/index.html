STREET BOXING

The StreetBarbellBOXING series is a set of popular boxing shells — bags, punch balls, various pillows for practicing punches. Boxing bags are specially designed for outdoor use. Reinforced PVC fabric is resistant to the environmental influence, has special elasticity and perfectly retains its properties at temperatures from -30 to +70 degrees. StreetBarbeeBOXING trainers are an excellent addition to outdoor sports grounds already equipped with StreetBarbell, StreetBarbellLight street exercise trainers, expanding their functionality, providing cardio load and attracting boxing fans to street training.
