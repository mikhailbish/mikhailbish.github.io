STREET BARBELL LIGHT

The StreetBarbellLight series of trainers is designed for the most convenient training, in almost any installation conditions. The stable design and the low center of gravity make them the optimal solution for organizing sports training complexes in parks, on beaches, as well as on open terraces. Thanks to well-thought-out biomechanics and the use of a patented load change system, it is easy and convenient to train on them. The line includes 15 trainers for all muscle groups and is equipped with a two-level system for changing the load with a weight change step of 2.5 kg. Clear design and unsurpassed reliability make StreetBarbellLight trainers the most popular equipment for small sports grounds.
