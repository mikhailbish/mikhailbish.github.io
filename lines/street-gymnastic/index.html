STREET GYMNASTIC

StreetGymnastic — bodyweight fitness equipment using as well aerobic load. They are perfect for works-out, stretching, warming up before performing more complex physical exercises, for maintaining muscle tone and cardio training. Due to their biomechanics, they do not load the joints and can be used by people of different age and levels of physical fitness. Due to the inability to increase the weight load, they cannot be used for strength development as it classifies them as fitness equipment.
