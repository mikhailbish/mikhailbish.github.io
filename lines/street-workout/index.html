STREET WORKOUT

The StreetWorkout line includes a set of crossbars, bars, handlebars, wall bars and other elements for pull-ups and push-ups. The complex was developed together with the worldwide workout founders and includes everything necessary for training fans of this type of street sports. It is also suitable for holding Workout competitions of various levels. The unique painting technology and all-metal steel locks for connecting elements make the StreetBarbell StreetWorkOut complex the most reliable and secure among competitors.
